    Best Answer:  It's perfectly normal to have a crush on a fictional/cartoon character, as long as it remains a harmless little crush. I've had crushes on fictional characters (including cartoon characters) before, and I'm a perfectly heathy, well-adjusted 18 year old.

    If it ever turns into something more than a harmless crush, and she forgets the person she has a crush on is just an animated fictional character, that is something more serious. But I highly doubt that will happen, because as I said, having crushes on fictional/cartoon characters is completely normal.

    Her having a crush on a female character may suggest she's gay or bisexual, not not necessarily. And even if your daughter is a lesbian, is that a problem? It shouldn't be. I suggest you just forget about it, as it's normal, and nothing to be so worried about.
